Cara Miller: Finding Community Through Art and Advocacy

Type: AP Format Profile | Published: Student Zine | Fall 2024

The Dangerous Escalation of Anti-Trans Healthcare Legislation

Type: News Analysis Blog Post | Fall 2024

An analysis of Texas Attorney General Ken Paxton's lawsuit against Dr. May Chi Lau for providing gender-affirming care to transgender minors, examining how this case represents an escalation from rhetorical "gender panics" to direct legal attacks on healthcare providers.
