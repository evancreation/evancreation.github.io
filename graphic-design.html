Informational & Data Visualization

Fear, Lies, and LGBTQ+ Rights: A Dangerous Alliance

Type: Informational Infographic | Tools: Canva, Adobe Illustrator

A comprehensive infographic examining the legislative landscape surrounding LGBTQ+ rights and the tactics of moral panic. The design presents complex data on anti-LGBTQ+ bills, methods of engineering fear, and community impact through clear visual hierarchy and accessible information design. Created for an academic Gender, Women's & Sexuality Studies course.

United States Presidential Ballot: The Election of 1860

Type: Historical Data Visualization | Tools: Canva, Adobe Photoshop

An educational infographic visualizing the 1860 presidential election through multiple data formats: ballot design, electoral map, pie chart, and candidate information. The cork board aesthetic creates visual interest while organizing complex historical information into digestible sections. Demonstrates ability to synthesize multiple data sources into cohesive visual storytelling.

Renters & Landlords Insurance Guide

Type: Educational Infographic | Tools: Canva

A practical guide breaking down renters and landlords insurance options with clear, accessible language. The design uses color-coding and structured layout to distinguish between renter and landlord perspectives while presenting coverage options, definitions, and cost comparisons. Created to make complex insurance information approachable for general audiences.
