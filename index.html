About

As a multimedia storyteller, I combine technical skills in video editing and audio production with a deep commitment to social impact storytelling. My work spans documentary film, audio narratives, graphic design, and both academic and creative writing.

Through my studies in Emergency Management & Business and Gender, Women's & Sexuality Studies, I bring an intersectional lens to storytelling, focusing on marginalized voices and community-centered narratives.

I'm currently seeking internship opportunities in AV editing and multimedia production, particularly with nonprofit organizations working toward social justice and community empowerment.
